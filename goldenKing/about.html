- Welcome -

Golden King general Imp. & Exp. Co., Ltd was established in December 2008. Over the past six years the growth and expansion of our company have given our team the necessary skills and experience to competently supply and distribute various kinds of machineries throughout several countries. A few of our expertise include machineries in field of woodwork , metalwork, food and beverage, agricultural , garments and shoes, office equipment, electronics and vehicle spare parts, etc.

In addition to this, we receive and deliver specific orders with desirable quantity and specification. Our services also include the complete setting up and running of all machinery for your desired new production plant with full support services.

Our machineries and spare parts are produced from a wide range of countries; particularly from Turkey, Italy, USA, Japan and China. To satisfy our costumers desire we have recently opened three new branches located in Hong Kong, Dubai and Guangzhou (China).

Additionally, we also have an agent situated in Addis Ababa (Ethiopia), which handles the distribution of our products in various African countries. All of our supplying materials are quality certified and warranted with free spare parts for one year. We believe our costumers deserve quality materials and the best services at negotiable prices, as a result this forms the basis for our mutual benefit by sealing the long-term relationship with our customers having constant positive feedback.
